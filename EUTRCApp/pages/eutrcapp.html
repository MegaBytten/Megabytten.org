About the app

The EUTRC App is an app that optimises attendance tracking, event management and player availability. It was crafted for Touch Rugby clubs, with functionality restricted to Exeter University Touch Rugby Club (EUTRC) specifically.

EUTRC App is the unofficial development name for the app, which has officially been released under the alias of "Touch Training Tracker"
